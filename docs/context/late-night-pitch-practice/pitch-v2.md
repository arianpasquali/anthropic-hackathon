# Klimaatkracht — Pitch v2

Source: late-night transcription (Apr 26). Latest canonical idea iteration.
Format: 5 chapters. ~90 seconds spoken. Each chapter = hook → substance → punchline.

---

## Chapter 1 — The Hidden Asset

**Hook (5s)**
> "155,000 Dutch people depend on foodbanks. Only 15% of who's eligible. Two reasons: not enough volunteers, not enough money."

**Substance (10s)**
> "But foodbanks aren't only social infrastructure. Pilot studies in Mexico and Spain measured it: for every 1 ton of CO₂ a foodbank emits, it prevents **32 tons** of emissions. Food rescued from landfill never becomes methane."

**Punchline**
> **"Foodbanks are the most efficient climate machines nobody is paying for."**

---

## Chapter 2 — The Gap

**Hook (5s)**
> "In the Netherlands, none of that climate impact is measured."

**Substance (10s)**
> "No infrastructure. No protocols. No revenue. Foodbanks track kilos and families — never carbon. Corporates need verifiable climate disclosures for CSRD starting 2026 — and they can't see this supply at all."

**Punchline**
> **"Two sides of the same gap. We close it."**

---

## Chapter 3 — The Method

**Hook (5s)**
> "Global Foodbanking Network already solved the math."

**Substance (10s)**
> "It's called FRAME — Food Recovery to Avoid Methane Emissions. Peer-reviewed. Validated abroad. Never applied to Dutch foodbanks. We ingest operational data — kilos dispersed, food categories, households served — and FRAME converts it into auditable CO₂e avoided."

**Punchline**
> **"We're not inventing carbon accounting. We're plugging foodbanks into it."**

---

## Chapter 4 — The Platform

**Hook (5s)**
> "Klimaatkracht. Two-sided marketplace. Live."

**Substance — show, don't tell (25s)**

| Beat | Screen | Line |
|------|--------|------|
| Marketplace | 180 foodbanks, three portfolios | "Climate-focus, social-focus, balanced." |
| Pick Rotterdam | 7,718 tCO₂e/yr, 4,160 t food rescued | "Real Voedselbank Rotterdam data." |
| Buy €25k package | Solvimon checkout → 600 tCO₂e attributed | "Money to foodbank. Disclosure to corporate." |
| Report streams | Claude generates ESRS E5 doc, live | "Audit-ready. Drops into the CSRD statement." |

**Punchline**
> **"Foodbanks get a new revenue stream. Corporates get a disclosure asset. Same euro."**

---

## Chapter 5 — The Business

**Hook (5s)**
> "Money flow is simple."

**Substance (10s)**
> "Corporates pay €41.67 per tCO₂e attributed. Foodbanks receive 100% of the package. We take **5%** platform fee on top. NL TAM: ~7,000 corporates under CSRD from 2026. 1% conversion at one package = **€1.75M ARR**."

**Traction (5s)**
> "12 foodbanks onboarded. 5 corporates committed. €425k pre-launch. Built this weekend on Claude."

**Closing punchline**
> **"Climate contribution. Not offsetting. Audit-defensible. Local-first."**
> **"We make impact real."**

## Delivery rules

- First sentence of every chapter = the hook. No throat-clearing.
- One number per chapter. More = jury tunes out.
- Punchline lands then **silence** — 1 second beat before next chapter.
- Demo silence at report reveal (Ch.4): 3 seconds. Let document materialize.
- Backup elevator if demo fails:
  > "Foodbank operations → audit-ready CSR climate disclosures. Corporates buy packages, foodbanks get funds, Claude generates ESRS reports. 12 banks, 5 corporates, €425k committed."
