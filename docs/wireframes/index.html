The calculation
FRAME formula — open and deterministic
The formula is identical for every food bank. No judgement calls. If you have the source data and emission factors, you can reproduce the result in a spreadsheet.
# CO₂e avoided per food category (kg) co2e_category = kg_rescued_category × ef_category × counterfactual_nl # Total CO₂e avoided (kg), sum across all categories co2e_total = Σ co2e_category — for c in {produce, bakery, dairy, meat, dry_goods, prepared, eggs} # NL counterfactual: incineration-with-energy-recovery vs US landfill default counterfactual_nl = 0.93 — RIVM Afvalmonitor 2024 + CBS Waste Statistics # Attribution to sponsor (economic pro-rata) sponsor_co2e = co2e_total × (sponsor_eur ÷ foodbank_annual_budget)
ef_category
FAO Food Wastage Footprint + WRAP + RIVM LCA data. Conservative (low) end of published ranges.
counterfactual_nl
0.93 vs US-default 1.0. NL waste is 72% incinerated (energy recovery), not landfilled. Makes claims conservative.
methodology_version
Stored on every FrameResult row. Quarterly review. Breaking changes increment version; reports re-generated.

⚠️ Conservative by design: Meat factor (8.5 kg CO₂e/kg) uses NL pork-dominant mix. Beef-heavy mix would push ~15 kg CO₂e/kg. We report the lower figure. Every claim errs toward understatement.
Dutch disposal counterfactual
NL-specific, not US-default
The standard FRAME model assumes US landfill as the counterfactual (high methane). The Netherlands uses predominantly incineration with energy recovery. Applying the NL-specific factor makes our estimates conservative relative to global defaults.
🇳🇱 Netherlands (used)
0.93
Incineration with energy recovery (72% of waste). RIVM Afvalmonitor 2024 + CBS Waste Statistics.
≠
🇺🇸 US default (not used)
1.00
Landfill assumed. Higher methane emissions. Would inflate NL CO₂e numbers — we choose not to use it.
What this means for an auditor: Any third-party recalculating our figures using global FRAME defaults will get a ~7% higher number. Our reports state the counterfactual used and its source. Sponsors reporting to CSRD reviewers should note that our factor is more conservative than the GFN default.
Per-value provenance
Every number carries its origin
Each extracted field in the data model stores a _source enum and a _method citation string. An auditor can reconstruct any figure to its origin.
Source types (SourceEnum)
extracted
Value read directly from the bank's annual report PDF. Claude cites page and table number in _method.
inferred_national_avg
NL national average applied when bank doesn't report the figure. Source is cited (e.g. CBS 2024).
inferred_calculation
Derived mathematically from other extracted values (e.g. weekly households × 52 = annual).
manual
Human-entered override. Logged with operator ID and timestamp. Triggers re-computation of all dependent fields.

For external auditors
What an ISAE 3000 reviewer needs — and where to find it
Sponsor reports are not subject to third-party assurance. The chain is designed so that limited or reasonable assurance can be obtained independently. Here is what a reviewer would check and where the evidence lives.
1
Source document PDF on file
Each food bank's annual report PDF is stored unmodified at the path referenced in AnnualReport.raw_file_path. Source URL links to the bank's own website. Hash of stored file matches published document.
2
Extraction trace _method field
Every numeric field stores its extraction method string: model, PDF page, table name, and the exact Dutch text it was parsed from. Reviewers can compare field values to the cited page in the original PDF.
3
FRAME calculation open formula
The formula on this page is complete and non-proprietary. FrameResult stores every intermediate: co2e per category, emission factor version, methodology version, computed_at timestamp. Reviewers can rerun the formula in a spreadsheet.
4
Attribution mechanics economic pro-rata
Attribution share = sponsor_eur ÷ foodbank_annual_budget. The annual budget figure carries its own _source and _method. Both sides of the ratio are auditable. The attribution_share field in every report is derived, not asserted.
5
Report generation model + timestamp
CSR reports are generated by Claude (model ID logged). The report references every source by section and page. CsrReport.generated_at and frame_result_id lock the report to a specific data snapshot — later updates do not silently alter a delivered report.

Common questions
Is this Scope 1, 2 or 3?
Scope 3 category 15 (investments) or category 1 depending on your value chain mapping. The ESRS E1-6 section documents the classification.
Can our auditor verify the numbers?
Yes. Enterprise tier includes full data export with all _source and _method fields. Starter/Standard can request on demand.
What if a food bank's data is late?
We use the prior year's annual report and flag the report as "preliminary". Final values are issued when new data arrives, with a delta note.
Is this certified carbon offsetting?
No — this is food rescue impact reporting, not carbon credit issuance. We are FRAME-aligned. Credits and offsets are a separate regulatory category.
