### Food Bank Marketplace Business Model

- Subscription model challenges for food banks
  - €30-200 subscription split across 100 food banks = €10 each
  - Individual product connection weaker with subscription approach
  - Platform reports vs direct engagement trade-offs
- Project-based alternative approach
  - Food banks request specific amounts (e.g., 50k for X project)
  - Cap set by association prevents runaway costs
  - Avoids subscription splitting issues

### Corporate CSR Integration

- Annual commitment structure makes most sense
  - Monthly subscriptions don’t align with CSR planning cycles
  - Revenue-based donation model: 1M ARR company donates 25k annually
  - Need system to optimize 25k distribution across marketplace

### Sustainability Data Translation

- CO2 certification opportunity
  - Companies provide funding, receive sustainability certification in return
  - Translation layer needed: existing data → framework data → CO2 credits
  - Marketplace eligibility requires open sustainability data
- Regulatory compliance angle
  - Companies obligated to report sustainability efforts
  - Food bank sustainability data could fulfill reporting requirements
  - Widens food bank mentioned as example case

### Technical Implementation

- Software layer development needed
  - Translates existing food bank data into standardized framework
  - Connects corporate funding to measurable sustainability outcomes
  - Platform determines optimal money distribution across food banks
